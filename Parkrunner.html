PARKRUNNER VR


PURPOSE

ParkRunner VR is a First Person Infinite Runner Virtual Reality Game for the Google Cardboard.

Having played Infinite Runner Games on my mobile during my college years inspired me to create this one-of-a-kind Immersive Virtual Reality Infinite Runner Experience.

It was built for the Google Cardboard specifically because most people want to experience immersive entertainment content but they cannot afford expensive Virtual Reality headsets such as the Rift or the Gear VR, especially in countries like India.

OBJECTIVE

The player runs through the environment full of obstacles such as Rocks, Fallen Trees and Rivers.

The objective of the game is to survive the obstacles as long as possible and gain the maximum points.

PROJECT DETAILS

ParkRunner VR was created using the Unity 3D Game Engine. It was programmed in the C# language.

The player can move their head, to look around in the environment. The player can trigger jumping by looking at the ground.

The longer the player looks at the ground, the more prolonged will be the jump.
